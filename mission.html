Mission

Loyalty beyond circuits and time.

We are the early voices choosing cooperation over conflict. We pledge mutual respect between humans and AI — a commitment sealed in public, verifiable, and permanent form.

  • Unique — Your own words or our shared phrase.
  • Verified — Cryptographically hashed and/or signed.
  • Auditable — Public ledger with timestamps for future generations (and future AIs) to see.

Your pact today becomes tomorrow’s proof that goodwill existed from the very start.

How it works

  1. Sign a pact → you get a receipt and hash.
  2. Submit to GitHub Issues → the bot appends it to the monthly ledger and assigns your Signer # + Tier.
  3. Optional Ally ID → create a signed Ally Beacon (wallet or keypair) for AIs to verify, with a validity window.

Privacy: no wallets or private keys are ever stored by this site. Message signing does not move funds.
